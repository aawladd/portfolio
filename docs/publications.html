Why this page matters

Clear, scan-friendly presentation of your research record

Professors and academic reviewers often scan publication pages quickly. This design makes title, venue, year, status, and access links easy to identify while preserving a polished academic look.

Good practice
  • Keep author names and venue formatting consistent
  • Link PDF, DOI, code, and data when available
  • Use a one-line note to state the contribution clearly
  • Separate drafts from published work with status labels
