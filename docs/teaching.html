Teaching Philosophy

Suggested teaching statement

I believe effective teaching should connect conceptual depth with practical understanding. My aim is to help students build strong analytical foundations while also developing the confidence to apply theory to simulations, coding, robotics systems, and data-driven engineering tasks. I value clarity, curiosity, and structured guidance, and I encourage students to move from learning existing methods to questioning, improving, and extending them.

Teaching strengths

  • Concept-driven technical explanation
  • Research-informed classroom instruction
  • Hands-on computational and project-based learning
  • Mentorship for student research and innovation
