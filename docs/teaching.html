Teaching philosophy

Connecting conceptual depth with practical understanding

I believe strong teaching should help students move from foundational understanding to independent technical thinking. My goal is to create learning environments where theory, computation, experimentation, and scientific reasoning support each other.

I value clarity, structured guidance, curiosity, and project-based exploration, especially in topics that connect mathematics, coding, robotics, simulation, and intelligent systems.

Teaching strengths
  • Concept-first technical explanation
  • Research-informed classroom design
  • Hands-on computational learning
  • Mentorship for student projects and inquiry
